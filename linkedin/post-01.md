## How to post

1. Upload `post-01-scorecard.png` as the image.
2. Paste the caption exactly as written above.
3. Do not let LinkedIn attach a link preview card. Paste `neverranked.com` as plain text in the body only — the algorithm suppresses posts with outbound link previews.
4. As comment 1 from your own account (within one minute of posting), paste a real screenshot of your own neverranked.com audit result. Sets the tone and shows you went first.
5. Pin the post for one week.

---

## Engagement plan

- **First 60 minutes:** reply to every commenter personally, within 5 minutes. LinkedIn's early engagement window is the whole game.
- **"Roast" replies:** when someone requests the roast, DM them three specific findings from a quick scan of their domain. Three bullets, 48 hours. Do not dump a 2,000-word audit.
- **Day 2-3:** repost the best commenter scorecards to your feed with a one-line observation each ("X scoring 3/4 makes sense, they were early on schema").
- **Day 7:** post a roll-up ("50 brands posted their scores, here's what I learned"). Becomes the next scheduled post and closes the loop.

## Why this passes the HM filters

- **Swap Test:** a generic agency couldn't run this mechanic. The ChatGPT mock + real audit tool + "roast" DM offer is specific to NR's stack.
- **Lever Test:** Contrast (old scoreboard vs new) + Confession (#1 Google wasn't in the list) + Specificity (dental, four names, 30 seconds, no email gate).
- **So What Test:** "your competitors are being handed customers while you rank #1" is a clear tension.
- **No AI filler:** no em dashes, no semicolons, no "revolutionary / unlock / transform" language.
