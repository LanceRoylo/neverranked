## Posting notes

- Drop one link per post maximum. LinkedIn suppresses posts with multiple links.
- Do not post the URL as a preview card. Paste it as plain text or put in comment one for better reach.
- Add 2-3 hashtags max per post if you use them: #AEO #SEO #AnswerEngineOptimization.
- Best time windows for B2B: Tue/Wed/Thu, 8-10am or 12-1pm in the target audience timezone.
