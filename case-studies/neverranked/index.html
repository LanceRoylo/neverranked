02 What We Built

Sixteen articles. Eight verticals. One playbook.

We followed the same phased approach we use with every client. Foundation first, then content clusters, then conversion infrastructure. Four weeks, no shortcuts, no exceptions.

Week 1 / Foundation
Full Organization, WebSite, BreadcrumbList schema deployed site-wide. Sitemap, RSS feed, and robots.txt with explicit AI crawler permissions for seven bots. Interactive ROI calculator with ten industry verticals.
Week 2 / Core Content
Six foundational articles: What Is AEO, How Long Does AEO Take, Schema Markup, Why ChatGPT Recommends Your Competitor, AEO Score, Website Traffic Dropped. Each with Article schema, FAQ markup, and internal cross-links to at least two other pages.
Week 3 / Vertical Expansion
Five industry verticals: dentists, med spas, lawyers, HVAC, real estate. Each with LocalBusiness-relevant schema examples and industry-specific conversion hooks tied to the audit funnel.
Week 4 / Conversion Layer
Five more articles: financial advisors, restaurants, chiropractors, Is SEO Dead, How to Check If Your Business Shows Up in ChatGPT. Free check tool live at check.neverranked.com. OG social preview images for all 16 articles. Full internal cross-linking audit -- every article links to at least two others.
