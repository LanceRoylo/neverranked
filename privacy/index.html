Privacy Policy

Last updated April 2026
01 Who We Are

Never Ranked (neverranked.com) is operated by Lance Roylo. If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at hello@neverranked.com.

02 Data We Collect

We collect the following categories of data:

  • Email addresses -- only when you voluntarily provide them (signing up, requesting an audit, etc.)
  • Domain URLs -- submitted through our AEO check tool for scanning
  • Anonymized IP hashes -- we hash IP addresses so they cannot be reversed to identify you
  • Usage analytics -- page views and scan events used to understand how the service is used
  • Cookies -- from Meta Pixel and LinkedIn Insight Tag for advertising purposes (see Section 07)
03 How We Use Your Data
  • To provide AEO scoring services and deliver scan results
  • To send email reports and nurture emails related to your account or scan activity
  • To improve the service based on aggregated usage patterns
  • To run retargeting campaigns via Meta and LinkedIn ad platforms
04 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Resend -- transactional and marketing email delivery
  • Stripe -- payment processing for audits and subscriptions
  • Cloudflare -- hosting, CDN, and security
  • Meta -- advertising pixel for retargeting on Facebook and Instagram
  • LinkedIn -- advertising pixel for retargeting on LinkedIn

We do not sell your data to third parties. These services receive only the data necessary to perform their function.

05 Data Retention
  • Scan events -- retained for 90 days, then deleted
  • Lead data (email, domain) -- retained for 365 days from last activity
  • Account data (paying customers) -- retained as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after cancellation
06 Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@neverranked.com. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

If you are located in the EU, UK, or California, you may also have the right to access, correct, or port your personal data. Contact us at the email above to exercise any of these rights.

07 Cookies

We use three categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies -- required for authentication and basic site functionality. These cannot be declined.
  • Analytics cookies -- used to track page views and usage patterns so we can improve the service.
  • Advertising cookies -- placed by Meta Pixel and LinkedIn Insight Tag for ad retargeting. You can decline these cookies when prompted.
08 Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
