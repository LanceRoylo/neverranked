About NeverRanked.

NeverRanked is a small research engagement. We measure what AI answer engines actually cite when someone asks about a brand, a competitor, or a category. The output is a forensic memo, not a dashboard, and a prepped punch list for the team executing the work. We do not make the changes ourselves.

What we measure

Seven AI surfaces, every day. Five citation-grade engines that search the live web (Perplexity, ChatGPT search, Gemini grounded, Microsoft Copilot via Bing, Google AI Overviews). Two model-knowledge engines that answer from training data alone (Claude, Gemma). Both layers measure different failure modes a brand can have. We track all seven across every category we run.

The boundary

We diagnose and we hand the executing team a clear set of moves. We do not write content, deploy schema, edit websites, or do any of the labor that converts a finding into a result. That separation is the position. Vendors who promise measurement and execution end up doing neither well, and the buyer never knows which lever produced the result.
