# Never Ranked. Social Calendar

Chronological queue. Most recent on top. Status flags:
`idea` → `draft` → `scheduled` → `posted` → `archived`.

## Conventions

- **Slug** matches the directory under `social/posts/`
- **Date** is the planned publish date, not the draft date
- **Platforms** is the comma list of where it ships
- **Lever** names the creative mechanism (Reframe, Specificity,
  Confession, Tension Hold, Format Surprise, Visible Mechanic)
- **Hook** is the first 80 chars of the caption, what stops the scroll
