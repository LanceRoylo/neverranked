## Engagement plan

- **Reply to every poll vote within 60 minutes.** Quick reply: "Most people say no. The Hawaii Theatre numbers (slide 3) explain why. Free check via the link on slide 4." Keeps viewers in the sequence.
- **DM anyone who taps the link sticker** with a one-line offer: "Saw you scanned. Happy to walk through the result if you want a second pair of eyes."

## What NOT to do

- **Don't post all 4 cards back-to-back in rapid succession.** Space them 10-30 seconds apart in the IG editor (the Stories tray auto-orders them).
- **Don't add music.** Reading content needs silence.
- **Don't @-tag Hawaii Theatre.** Same logic as the feed posts.
- **Don't use "swipe up" language.** Instagram retired swipe-up; the link sticker is the only way.
