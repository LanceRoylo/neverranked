# What this is

A 4-card Instagram Stories sequence. General AEO category education, no client references. Three jobs at once:

1. **Engagement signal:** Card 1's poll generates rapid taps; the IG algorithm reads as Story-worth.
2. **Education:** Cards 2 and 3 teach what schema is and how it works.
3. **Conversion:** Card 4 carries the link sticker to check.neverranked.com.

Stories outperform feed posts for driving link clicks (Stories have direct link stickers; feed posts have link-in-bio friction).

# The sequence

| Card | Purpose | Sticker / Interaction |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Hook + poll | Poll: "YES (have it dialed in) / NO (have not heard the term)" |
| 2 | Confession + mechanism | None - tension hold |
| 3 | The five-most-missing schema types | None - educational meat |
| 4 | CTA | Link sticker -> check.neverranked.com |

Each card auto-advances after 15 seconds. Total viewer time investment: ~60 seconds.

# Engagement plan

- **Post 30 minutes after the feed post** so feed viewers see Stories activity from you when they're already in your profile.
- **Reply to every poll vote within 60 minutes.** Quick reply: "Tap card 3 for the five most-missing schema types. Free check on slide 4." Re-engages viewers across the sequence.
- **DM anyone who taps the link sticker** with: "Saw you scanned. Happy to walk through the result if you want a second pair of eyes."
- **Save the Story sequence to a Highlight named 'AEO 101'** so it stays on the profile.

# What NOT to do

- **Don't include music.** Reading content. Music fights comprehension.
- **Don't tease "swipe up" -- Instagram retired swipe-up.** Link sticker only.
- **Don't post more than 4 cards.** Completion rate drops sharply after 5 Stories from the same account in one session.
- **Don't reference a specific client.** This is general category education.
