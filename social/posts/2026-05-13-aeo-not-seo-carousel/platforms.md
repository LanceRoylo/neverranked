## Posting notes

- **Wait at least 24h after the Tuesday single-image post** so the algorithm sees them as separate content, not a spam cluster.
- **Educational carousels are save-magnets.** The "slide 5 has the five schema types" call-out in the caption is engineered to force a slow scroll-through.
- **Carousels rank for first-comment-within-30-minutes** more than single images do. Be present.
- **LinkedIn cross-post version**: LinkedIn carousels are PDFs uploaded as documents. Export the 6 PNGs into a single 6-page PDF (Preview > Export as PDF works), upload as document, repurpose the caption.

## What NOT to do

- **Don't paid-boost a carousel.** Save-driven content does worse with paid (the algorithm assumes paid = lower quality and de-weights).
- **Don't add an audio track if you make it a Reel version.** This is reading content. Audio fights the comprehension.
- **Don't tag Hawaii Theatre.** Same logic as the single-image post.
- **Don't add emojis to the slide visuals.** Restraint is the brand.
