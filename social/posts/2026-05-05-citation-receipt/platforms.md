# Platforms · citation receipts

## Instagram · primary

- **Format:** Single feed post, vertical 1080 × 1350
- **Hero:** Vertical receipts stack
- **Caption:** Instagram block from caption.md
- **Hashtags:** Eight max, on their own line at end of caption
- **Story:** Reshare the feed post to story. Add a poll sticker:
  "Has your brand been cited by an AI engine in the last week?
  YES / I have no idea." Engagement bait that is also honest.
- **Link:** Bio link → check.neverranked.com

## LinkedIn · carousel

- **Format:** 5-slide carousel, one card per slide. Slide 1 is
  the headline. Slides 2-6 are the engine cards. Slide 7 is the
  CTA: "check.neverranked.com"
- **Caption:** LinkedIn block from caption.md
- **Hashtags:** None
- **Link:** First comment

## X / Threads · same day

- **Format:** Single post with image attachment (one combined
  card, not the carousel)
- **Hero:** Vertical version cropped to fit
- **Caption:** X / Threads block from caption.md

## Reusability note

This is a template, not a one-off. Run a citation-receipts post
weekly. Each one rotates the prompt and the cited domains. The
audience learns to expect the format. Over time, the receipts
become the brand asset.
