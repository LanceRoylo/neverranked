# Alt text

A dark editorial graphic showing five citation cards stacked
vertically, one for each AI engine: ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini,
Google AI Overviews, and Claude. Each card shows a sample prompt
in monospace text, a short answer paragraph with a cited domain
underlined in gold, and a status tag at the bottom reading either
CITED or MISSING. Three cards are tagged CITED, two are tagged
MISSING. At the top of the image, a gold italic Playfair line
reads: Five engines. One answer. You are cited or you are
invisible.
