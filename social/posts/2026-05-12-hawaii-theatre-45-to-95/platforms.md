## What NOT to do

- **Don't add emojis.** Brand voice is restrained.
- **Don't boost paid until you see 48h of organic baseline.**
- **Don't mention specific competitor tools by name** (Profound, Athena, etc.) in the caption. Save that for follow-up comments when someone asks "how is this different from X?"
- **Don't reuse this format every week.** Single-card comparison cards work because they're surprising. Cadence: max once per 3 weeks.
