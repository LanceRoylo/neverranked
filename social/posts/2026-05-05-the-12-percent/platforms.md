# Platforms: the 1.2% post

## Instagram (Lance, personal) · primary

- **Format:** Single feed post, 4:5 portrait (1080 × 1350)
- **Hero:** `hero-instagram.png` (generated locally via Pillow,
  brand fonts, 1080 × 1350)
- **Caption:** Instagram (Lance, personal) block from caption.md
- **Hashtags:** Seven, on own line:
  `#AEO #AnswerEngineOptimization #AISearch #SchemaMarkup #SEO2026 #SmallBusinessSEO #AIcitations`
- **Link:** Bio link points at check.neverranked.com on the day
  this post is live
- **Story reshare:** Reshare the feed post to Lance's personal
  IG story within 30 minutes. Add a question sticker:
  "Has your traffic dropped while your Google rank held steady?
  YES / NO / I have no idea." Drives engagement and surfaces
  self-identified prospects.
- **First-hour engagement:** Reply to every comment within 5
  minutes. The IG algorithm reads early replies as a meaningful
  interaction signal and pushes the post wider.

## LinkedIn (Lance, personal) · same week

- **Format:** Single image post (the same `hero-instagram.png`
  works at 4:5 for LinkedIn too)
- **Caption:** LinkedIn (Lance, personal) block from caption.md
- **Hashtags:** None (LinkedIn personal posts are not rewarded
  for hashtag use)
- **Link:** Drop `check.neverranked.com` parenthetically in the
  caption, OR in the first comment if you prefer maximum reach
- **Tag:** Tag the Never Ranked company page in the caption
  (helps the company page get incidental reach off your post)
- **Spacing:** Post LinkedIn at least 2-3 days apart from the
  Instagram version so they read as separate observations
  rather than cross-posted content

## Facebook (Lance, personal) · same week

- **Format:** Single image post, 4:5 portrait (same
  `hero-instagram.png`, ships natively to FB feed)
- **Caption:** Facebook (Lance, personal) block from caption.md
- **Hashtags:** None
- **Link:** No outbound link in the post body. The "DM me" close
  routes interested friends to a one-on-one conversation. If
  someone asks for the link in comments, drop
  check.neverranked.com there.
- **First-hour engagement:** Reply to every friend comment
  within 5 minutes. Tag 1-2 specific friends in the comments who
  might have something to say (NEVER mass tag).
- **Spacing:** Space at least 2 days from the Instagram and
  LinkedIn versions. Posting all three same-day reads as
  cross-promotion and dilutes each.

## Reusability note

The single-stat visual treatment is a template, not a one-off.
Future posts can reuse the same Pillow generation pattern with a
different number, eyebrow, and kicker:

- "1.2 percent" (this post), what AI Overviews do to blue links
- "45 to 95", Hawaii Theatre AEO transformation
- "Six engines", multi-engine citation tracking
- Any other single dramatic number Never Ranked surfaces

Run them as a series, weekly or every other week. The repeating
visual DNA compounds the brand asset.
