# Visual brief · ranking #1, traffic zero

## Concept

The paradox in one image. Two numbers, side by side, under a
single Playfair italic line. Nothing else.

## Layout

- Aspect ratio: 1080 × 1080 for IG, 1200 × 630 for LinkedIn (two versions)
- Background: `#121212` deep charcoal with subtle film grain overlay
- Subtle vignette at corners

## Composition

Top: small Barlow Condensed eyebrow, ALL CAPS, `.18em` tracking,
gold `#e8c767`, reads `A REAL CUSTOMER · APRIL 2026`.

Center, two columns split by a hairline gold rule:

- LEFT column header in Barlow ALL CAPS cream `.18em`: `AVERAGE POSITION`
  Below it, in Playfair Display gold italic at huge scale: `15.1`
  Below that, mono `#fbf8ef` smaller: `Best in his category.`

- RIGHT column header in Barlow ALL CAPS cream `.18em`: `ESTIMATED TRAFFIC`
  Below it, in Playfair Display cream regular at huge scale: `0`
  Below that, mono `#fbf8ef` smaller: `Same period.`

Bottom: single Playfair italic line, gold, mid-sentence emphasis:
`He is winning the leaderboard. *Losing the customer.*`

Smallest possible NeverRanked wordmark in lower-right corner.

## Visual rules

- No emojis. No clip-art. No trending fonts.
- Type carries the message. No illustrations needed.
- The two numbers should feel like a printed manifest, not a
  SaaS infographic. Generous negative space between elements.
- Test: cover the wordmark. The post should still feel like
  Never Ranked from the italic Playfair, gold-on-dark, mono
  treatment, and editorial restraint alone.
