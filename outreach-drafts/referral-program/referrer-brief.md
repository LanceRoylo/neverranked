# Referrer brief — for Katy

A short guide so you can introduce NeverRanked confidently without having to learn or sell the product. The whole idea is that you open the door and Lance does the rest.

## What NeverRanked is, in plain terms

More and more people find businesses by asking AI now, not just Google. They ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google's AI answers things like "best personal injury lawyer in Tampa" or "good CPA near me," and the AI names a few specific businesses.

NeverRanked measures whether the AI names a given business when its customers ask those buying questions, shows which competitors get named instead, and helps the business become one of the ones the AI recommends. It is measurement and research, done rigorously. The honesty of the measurement is the whole point, which is why the way we talk about it matters.

## How the referral works

You introduce Lance to a decision-maker you know who might be a fit. That is it. You do not have to explain how it works, quote a price, or make any promises. Lance takes the introduction and handles the qualifying, the pitch, the pricing, and all the delivery. You stay hands-off.

## How to make a good introduction (and what to avoid)

**Do:**
- Introduce Lance to the right person: the owner, the managing partner, the head of marketing. Someone who can decide.
- Frame it simply and warmly: "I know someone who measures how AI search engines recommend businesses in your space. Given how people are starting to find [lawyers / accountants / your category], I thought it was worth a conversation."
- Keep it to: "Want me to connect you two?" Then make the intro and step back.

**Don't:**
- Don't promise results or say they will "rank #1" or "get more clients." We measure, we don't promise outcomes.
- Don't quote prices.
- Don't tell them they are "invisible to AI" or name who beats them. That is a real finding, but it is Lance's to present with the actual data behind it. If you say it casually and it turns out to be more nuanced, it makes everyone look less credible.
- Don't try to explain the methodology. You don't need to, and a half-explanation creates more questions than it answers.

## Why the light touch

The product's entire value is that the measurement is rigorous and honest. The fastest way to undercut that is for it to get oversold before the data is on the table. You opening the door warmly, and Lance walking in with the proof, is the version that lands and protects the credibility the whole thing runs on. It also keeps your part easy: you never have to defend a claim you made.

## What you get

See the agreement for the full terms. Short version: for each introduction that becomes a paying customer, you get a one-time cut of their setup fee plus a monthly cut for as long as they stay a customer. Per market. It is meant to be real, recurring income, not a token.
