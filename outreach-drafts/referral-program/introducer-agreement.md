**Introducer Agreement**

This agreement is between [NEVERRANKED LLC], a [STATE] limited liability company ("NeverRanked"), and [KATY FULL NAME] ("Introducer"), effective [DATE].

**1. What this is.**
Introducer makes warm introductions between NeverRanked and businesses that might become customers. That is the whole of Introducer's role. NeverRanked handles everything after the introduction: qualifying, pricing, pitching, delivery, and the customer relationship.

**2. What the Introducer does, and does not do.**
Introducer makes introductions to decision-makers Introducer knows. Introducer does not pitch the service, quote prices, describe the methodology, promise results, make claims about a prospect's current AI visibility, or agree to anything on NeverRanked's behalf. Introducer has no authority to bind NeverRanked. Opening the door is the job; NeverRanked carries the conversation from there.

**3. The fee.**
For each prospect that Introducer introduces and that becomes a paying NeverRanked customer, NeverRanked will pay Introducer, per category the customer subscribes to:

- A one-time fee of **20% of the kickoff fee**, paid within [15] days after the customer's kickoff payment clears.
- A recurring fee of **10% of the monthly subscription**, paid monthly within [15] days after each of the customer's monthly payments clears, for as long as the customer remains active.

A customer that runs multiple categories or markets pays a separate kickoff and monthly for each, and the fees above apply to each.

**4. What counts as "introduced."**
A prospect is "introduced" by Introducer if Introducer made the first warm contact between that prospect and NeverRanked, and the prospect was not already in NeverRanked's active pipeline at that time. If there is ever a question, the parties will resolve it in good faith. Introductions are credited to the Introducer who made them.

**5. No obligations, no exclusivity.**
Introducer is under no obligation to make any introductions and has no quota. NeverRanked is under no obligation to pursue any introduced prospect. Neither party is exclusive to the other.

**6. Independent, not a partner or employee.**
Introducer is an independent party, not an employee, partner, agent, or representative of NeverRanked. This agreement does not create a partnership, joint venture, or employment relationship. Introducer is responsible for any taxes on fees received.

**7. Term and ending it.**
Either party may end this agreement at any time with written notice. If the agreement ends, recurring fees already owed on customers Introducer previously introduced continue to be paid for as long as those customers remain active, under the terms above. [OPTIONAL ALTERNATIVE: recurring fees continue for [24] months after termination, then stop.]

**8. Confidentiality.**
Each party will keep the other's non-public information (customer details, pricing specifics, methods) confidential and use it only for this arrangement.

**9. Governing law.**
This agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE].
