# NeverRanked Certified Practitioner

A practical certification program for marketing operators who deploy
and maintain Answer Engine Optimization for clients. Built on the
methodology NeverRanked uses to ship customer work, codified so any
trained practitioner can apply it consistently.

## Why this exists

The AEO category is being staffed by people who learned SEO five
years ago and added "AI" to their title last quarter. Most agency
teams have no formal training in:

- Schema.org structured data deployment that survives compliance review
- Multi-engine citation tracking with statistical attribution
- The Clarity Principle voice that distinguishes audit deliverables
  customers actually act on from "expert" reports they file away
- llms.txt curation, agent-readiness schema, and the patterns
  emerging in late 2026
- The vertical-specific constraints (banking compliance, healthcare
  HIPAA, legal bar rules) that determine whether work ships at all

NeverRanked Certified Practitioner closes the gap. It's the training
program we wish had existed when we built our own playbook, and we're
opening it to the operators who will deliver AEO work for the next
five years.

## Who this is for

- Marketing agency teams that have signed (or are evaluating) a
  NeverRanked wholesale partnership
- In-house SEO/AEO teams at companies running their own programs
- Freelance specialists who want a credential that distinguishes
  "knows AEO" from "watched a YouTube video about AI search"
- Web developers and CMS specialists who deploy schema as part of
  their work

## What it covers

### Module 1: Foundations of AEO (3 hours)

- The seven-engine citation surface and how each engine retrieves
- Schema.org as the substrate, and what AI actually reads
- The 0-100 AEO score: methodology, components, weighting
- Diagnostic vs deployment: why most "AEO tools" stop at the audit
- Where AEO differs from SEO and where the disciplines compound

### Module 2: Schema Deployment (5 hours)

- Organization, WebSite, BreadcrumbList: the citation foundation
- Vertical-specific primary types (FinancialService, LocalBusiness,
  RealEstateAgent, MedicalOrganization, EducationalOrganization)
- FAQPage, Article, HowTo: the content-citation surface
- AggregateRating and Review: social proof for AI engines
- Validation, error handling, and the snippet-vs-static deployment
  decision

### Module 3: Compliance-Aware Schema (3 hours)

- Financial services: FDIC/NCUA, Truth in Savings, Equal Housing
  Lender, NMLS disclosures inside JSON-LD
- Healthcare: HIPAA-aware patterns, what NOT to expose
- Legal: state bar marketing rules, unauthorized practice of law
- The customer compliance review workflow and how to pass it on
  the first attempt

### Module 4: Citation Tracking and Attribution (4 hours)

- Building a vertical prompt corpus that reflects real buyer queries
- Web-grounded vs API-only engine querying
- Statistical attribution: detecting whether deployments moved
  citation share at p < 0.05
- Industry benchmarks and percentile reporting
- Recognizing engine behavior changes within the same week they
  happen

### Module 5: The Clarity Principle (2 hours)

- The three-layer pattern: outcome headline, platform-specific
  where-to-fix, collapsed technical detail
- Voice that customers actually act on
- Anti-patterns that signal AI-written content (em-dashes, "not
  just X but Y", formulaic transitions)
- Audit deliverables that pass executive review

### Module 6: llms.txt and Agent Readiness (3 hours)

- The llms.txt standard, scoring rubric, and engine adoption
- Agent task surfaces: ReserveAction, ApplyAction, BuyAction,
  ContactAction
- Vertical templates and what should never be exposed as an
  agent action
- Where the agent ecosystem is heading through 2027

### Module 7: Customer Engagement (2 hours)

- The 90-day playbook: Phase 1 deployment, Phase 2 measurement,
  Phase 3 expansion
- Reading the dashboard for trend detection vs noise
- Handling customer objections (will this hurt SEO, what if the
  schema is wrong, what does this cost long-term)
- The wholesale-margin economics and how to price for the
  customer

### Module 8: Capstone Audit (4 hours)

The candidate runs a full NeverRanked audit on a designated test
site (a real Hawaii business we own the schema for). Submission
includes:

- Executive summary in Clarity Principle voice
- Technical audit identifying 8+ findings
- Schema review with paste-ready JSON-LD for 5+ types
- 90-day roadmap with prioritized milestones
- Delivery email drafted at sending standard

The capstone is scored against the rubric NeverRanked uses on its
own production audits.

## Time and format

- Total program: ~26 hours of self-paced content + the capstone
- Available formats: async video + readings, or a 3-day intensive
  cohort in Honolulu (limited seats)
- Capstone deadline: 30 days after Module 8 unlocks
- Re-attempt allowed once at no additional cost

## Certification standard

A passing score on the capstone (75 of 100 against the rubric) plus
a verified deployment of all six learning-objective schema types on
a live site of the candidate's choice. NeverRanked publishes a
public-facing roster of certified practitioners. Certification is
revocable for documented violations of the operating principles.
