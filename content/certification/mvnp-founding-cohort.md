## Why this matters for MVNP

Three concrete benefits:

### 1. Talent retention and hiring leverage

A certified practitioner is a credentialed AEO specialist. MVNP can
post roles with the credential as a requirement, market the team's
credential to new business prospects, and use the program as a
retention tool ("we invest in your skills").

### 2. Pricing leverage on AEO retainers

A certified team supports higher hourly rates than an uncertified
one. The market expectation in 2026-2027 will be that AEO work is
done by trained specialists. MVNP becomes an early-credentialed shop
in the Pacific region — alongside maybe one or two mainland firms —
and prices accordingly.

### 3. Referral pipeline

The public roster of certified practitioners is the de-facto
referral network for businesses outside MVNP's existing footprint.
When NeverRanked declines a direct customer because we are at
capacity OR the business is in MVNP's exclusive territory, we route
them to MVNP's certified practitioners.

## Curriculum customized for MVNP

Two of the eight modules are tailored to the Hawaii financial
services exclusivity:

### Module 3 (Compliance-Aware Schema) — Hawaii Banking Track

Adds Hawaii state-specific banking regulation review:

- State-chartered vs federally-chartered Hawaii institutions and
  the schema differences (state insurance disclaimer language vs FDIC)
- Hawaii Department of Commerce and Consumer Affairs (DCCA) rules
  affecting financial advertising
- The four-bank case study (ASB, FHB, BOH, CPB): what each is doing
  well, what each is missing, and the deployment path that works
  inside their respective compliance review windows

### Module 7 (Customer Engagement) — Hawaii Banking Track

Adds the customer-engagement playbook specific to Hawaii regional
banks:

- The Hawaii buyer journey for personal and business banking
- The Hawaii-specific competitive prompt corpus (the 42-prompt set
  documented in `content/prompt-corpus/hawaii-community-banking.md`)
- The seasonal cycles in Hawaii banking marketing (tax-time CDs,
  spring home-buying, summer SBA pushes)

## Capstone — fast-track using ASB pilot data

If ASB consents, MVNP practitioners can use the ASB pilot
deployment as their capstone subject. Two operational benefits:

1. The capstone work IS production work for ASB. No throwaway
   effort.
2. The MVNP team's certification work is reviewed by Lance against
   the same rubric we apply to NeverRanked's own delivery, which
   means MVNP's first ASB deliverables are pre-reviewed at the same
   standard as our retail work.

If ASB does not consent or signs separately, MVNP practitioners use
a designated test site (a NeverRanked-owned Hawaii business we
provision specifically for the cohort). Same rubric applies.
