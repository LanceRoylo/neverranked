### Post 6 -- The Schema Reality
**Maps to:** /blog/schema-markup-ai-search/
**Creative lever:** Contrast
**Goal:** Technical authority, attract SEO-adjacent audience

Most schema markup guides are written for Google rich snippets.

AI models parse structured data differently. And the schema types that drive AI citations are not the same ones that generate star ratings in search results.

The three that matter most for AI visibility:
Organization schema with complete entity data.
LocalBusiness schema with service area and hours.
FAQPage schema that mirrors the exact questions the model predicts.

The ones most agencies spend time on that AI models largely ignore: Recipe, Event, and most Product markup when it is not connected to a review aggregate.

If your schema strategy was built for Google in 2022, it is optimized for the wrong machine.

### Post 10 -- What Is AEO (The Foundational Post)
**Maps to:** /blog/what-is-aeo/
**Creative lever:** Reframe
**Goal:** Define the category, establish thought leadership

There is a new acronym in marketing and most agencies cannot explain it yet.

AEO. Answer Engine Optimization.

It is the practice of getting your business cited by AI engines. Not ranked. Cited. Named as a recommendation when someone asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI Overviews a question your business should be the answer to.

The difference between SEO and AEO is not subtle. SEO optimizes for ranking position on a results page. AEO optimizes for being included in the answer itself. Different inputs, different signals, different measurement.

The businesses that figure this out in 2026 will have a structural advantage that compounds. The ones that wait until 2027 will be playing catch-up against competitors who already own the citation share.
