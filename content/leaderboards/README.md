# Leaderboards

Public-ranking artifacts. Currently INTERNAL only. None have been
published.

## Why these exist

Public AEO leaderboards are a moat asset. Every weekly update creates
indexable content competitors cannot fake without running our audit
pipeline. Each ranked entity has a reason to engage with us.

## Publication policy

Leaderboards are not published until:

1. The category lead has been previewed privately and given 14 days
   to opt into a Signal contract before going live
2. The methodology page has been reviewed and is publishable
3. The scores have been independently verified twice (first scan,
   second scan 24h later, scores within ±5 points)

## Files

- `methodology.md` — how the AEO score is computed and what each
  component measures. Public when published.
- `hawaii-community-banking-2026-05.md` — first leaderboard. Built
  from live data on May 7, 2026. Status: internal, not published.

## After ASB meeting

If ASB signs the Signal pilot, give them 14 days, then publish.
If ASB declines, give them 14 days, publish anyway. The leaderboard
is structural — it exists whether they sign or not.
