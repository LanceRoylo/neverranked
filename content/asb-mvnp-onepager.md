## Why this is a 90-day decision, not a 12-month decision

AI models retrain on cycles. Schema and content deployed in month one gets absorbed into the next training window. It takes ~90 days for ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews to consistently recognize and cite a new entity.

Our 3-month minimum exists to protect customers from quitting before the work has time to compound. Any sooner and the data is noise. Any longer is unnecessary commitment.
