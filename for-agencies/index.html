§03 How the partnership works

Four steps. You own the client.

01

You introduce, we onboard

You send us the client, we handle technical setup in 48 hours. You stay the account lead. We do not contact the client without you.

02

We execute the work

Schema, citations, content briefs, and monitoring. Every recommendation runs through the dashboard the client already sees, so nothing is hidden.

03

You forward the brief

Weekly score change, new citations, competitor movement, and this week's actions. Plain English, forward-ready. You send it under your brand every Monday.

04

You upsell and renew

You set the retail price. You bill the client. We bill you. If the client leaves, they leave your agency, not ours, because we were never in the room.
