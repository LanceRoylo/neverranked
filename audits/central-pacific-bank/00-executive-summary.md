## One-page executive summary

Central Pacific Bank is a Hawaii-based financial institution serving personal and business banking customers across the state. The homepage is content-rich (1,614 words), has a solid title tag, and demonstrates clear messaging around the "We Got You" brand position. The site structure appears to be enterprise-grade with 306 internal links and strong topical depth.

But the technical SEO and AI optimization foundation is entirely absent. Zero structured data of any kind. No social preview tags. No canonical tag. No meta description. Eight competing H1 tags fighting for hierarchy. 34 of 56 images missing alt text. When ChatGPT is asked about Hawaii banks, CPB has given it nothing to cite. When the homepage is shared on LinkedIn, it shows up as a broken link preview. Google has no Organization schema to build a Knowledge Panel from. This is a clean-slate scenario. The content and brand are strong. The machine-readable layer that makes it all discoverable is completely missing.

**Headline findings:**

1. 0 of 1 sampled pages have any structured data (no Organization schema, no WebSite schema, no BreadcrumbList). AI engines and Google Knowledge Graph have no entity record for Central Pacific Bank.
2. 0 of 1 sampled pages have Open Graph or Twitter Card tags. Every social share and AI citation shows a blank preview card.
3. 0 of 1 sampled pages have a canonical tag. Search engines cannot determine the preferred URL if www and non-www versions both resolve.
4. The homepage has 8 competing H1 tags. Heading hierarchy is broken, confusing both accessibility tools and search crawlers.
5. 34 of 56 images on the homepage are missing alt text (61% gap). The site is partially invisible to screen readers and image search.
6. The homepage has no meta description. Google is auto-generating snippets from body content, often poorly.
7. 65 external links on the homepage (average per page). High outbound link density may dilute internal link equity if not intentional.
8. No BreadcrumbList schema detected. Rich result breadcrumbs in Google SERPs are disabled.

**What's already working:**
- Title tag is in the optimal range (34 characters) and includes brand + value proposition ("Central Pacific Bank - We Got You!")
- Content depth is strong (1,614 words on homepage). Well above thin content threshold.
- Internal linking is robust (306 internal links). Site demonstrates strong information architecture.
- Word count and topical coverage suggest the site has material to support rich schema markup (FAQPage, Service, FinancialProduct).

**The 90-day target:** Organization and WebSite schema live site-wide. Clean social preview cards on all key pages. Single H1 per page. Alt text on 100% of images. Canonical tags deployed across all templates. CPB becomes cite-able by AI engines and visible in Google Knowledge Graph.

**The single most important action for this week:** Deploy Organization schema (BankOrCreditUnion type) via the NeverRanked snippet. This is the foundational entity signal that unlocks everything else.

## Methodology

This audit was produced using the Never Ranked audit methodology:

1. **Intake:** Fetched the site, robots.txt, sitemap.xml. Analyzed URL structure.
2. **Sample selection:** Pulled 1 representative page (homepage) for foundational technical and schema review.
3. **Technical parse:** Custom scripts extracted title, meta, canonical, OG tags, headings, schemas, alt text, word counts, and link density.
4. **Schema parse:** Full JSON-LD block inspection with type extraction (none found on sampled page).
5. **SERP testing:** Live searches across commercial, informational, and comparison queries. Captured top 10 results and AI-synthesized summaries.
6. **Competitor fetch:** Raw HTML of 3-5 direct competitors. Same analysis.
7. **Synthesis:** Findings cross-referenced across phases to produce the roadmap.
