Where ChatGPT Excels

ChatGPT is the most versatile AI text generator available. It can write anything from emails to code to creative fiction. For agents who need a general-purpose AI assistant for tasks beyond listing content, ChatGPT is a strong choice.

ChatGPT also has a generous free tier and a large ecosystem of plugins and integrations. If you are already paying for ChatGPT Plus for other business tasks, using it for listing descriptions has zero marginal cost.

The caveat is that you get what you prompt for. Without careful prompting, ChatGPT produces generic real estate copy that sounds like every other AI-generated listing on the MLS.

Where Montaic Excels

Montaic is purpose-built for real estate agents. It learns your voice during onboarding, checks every output for Fair Housing compliance, respects your MLS character limits, and generates 11 content types from a single property input.

The difference shows up in the output. ChatGPT requires you to engineer prompts for each listing and manually check compliance. Montaic does that work automatically, every time, without you thinking about it.

For agents who write 10+ listings per month, the time savings alone justify the switch. For agents who care about voice consistency across their brand, there is no comparison.

Who Should Choose What

Choose ChatGPT if you need a general AI assistant and only occasionally write listing descriptions. The free tier is sufficient for low-volume use, and you can invest time in building custom prompts.

Choose Montaic if listing content is a core part of your workflow. If you write multiple listings per month, need consistent voice across your brand, or want MLS compliance and social content without extra prompting, Montaic saves time and produces better results.

The Bottom Line

ChatGPT is a powerful general-purpose tool, but it requires significant prompting to produce real estate content that meets MLS standards, avoids Fair Housing violations, and sounds like your writing. Montaic does all of that automatically because it is built for one job: helping real estate agents create listing content faster.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is ChatGPT free for real estate agents?
ChatGPT has a free tier with limited capabilities and a Plus tier at $20/month. However, neither tier includes real estate-specific features like MLS compliance checking, Fair Housing language scanning, or voice matching. You would need to build and maintain custom prompts for real estate use.
Can ChatGPT learn my writing voice?
ChatGPT has a memory feature that retains some context across conversations, but it does not perform the structured voice analysis that Montaic uses. Montaic analyzes your past listings during onboarding to build a persistent voice profile that shapes every generation.
Does ChatGPT check for Fair Housing compliance?
No. ChatGPT does not include automated Fair Housing language checking. Agents using ChatGPT for listing descriptions need to manually review every output for potential violations. Montaic scans every generation automatically before showing you the result.
Can I use both Montaic and ChatGPT?
Yes. Many agents use Montaic for listing-specific content and ChatGPT for general business tasks like email drafting, brainstorming, and research. The tools serve different purposes and complement each other.
