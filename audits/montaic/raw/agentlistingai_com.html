Write Listings That Sell

Great real estate listings do more than describe a property—they tell a story that helps buyers imagine their future home. Here's how to get the most from AgentListingAI:

🎯 Be Specific in Your Notes

Instead of "nice kitchen," try "white quartz counters with waterfall edge, Wolf range, pot filler." Specific details create vivid mental images that generic descriptions can't match.

🎤 Use Voice Dictation

Walk through the property and describe what you see out loud. Your phone's speech-to-text captures natural, authentic observations that read better than typed notes.

✨ Match Tone to Price Point

Luxury homes deserve elevated language. Starter homes benefit from warmth and approachability. Use the Tone selector to match your market segment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is AgentListingAI really free?
Yes, completely free. No credit card, no subscription, no hidden fees. We believe every agent deserves access to professional-quality listing copy, regardless of budget. Generate as many listings as you need.
Which listing length should I choose?
Short (MLS) is ideal for character-limited MLS fields—punchy, under 900 characters. Medium works great for Zillow, Realtor.com, and website listings. Long (Brochure) creates detailed narratives perfect for luxury print materials and property websites.
How do I get the best results?
The more detail you provide in the "Property Story" field, the better your listing. Try using voice dictation on your phone while walking through the property—describe what you see, how the light feels, what makes it special. Our AI transforms your raw notes into polished copy.
Is the AI-generated content Fair Housing compliant?
Our AI is designed to avoid protected class language and discriminatory phrases. For a deeper understanding, see our Fair Housing compliance guide. However, we always recommend reviewing your listings before publishing. You know your local regulations best—use our output as a strong starting point, then apply your professional judgment.
Can I use these listings on MLS, Zillow, and my website?
Absolutely. The descriptions you generate are yours to use anywhere—MLS, Zillow, Realtor.com, Redfin, your brokerage website, print brochures, social media, email campaigns. No attribution required.
Do you save my property information?
Your listing history is saved locally in your browser only—it never leaves your device unless you choose to copy and share it. We don't store your property details on our servers. Clear your browser data anytime to remove saved listings.
