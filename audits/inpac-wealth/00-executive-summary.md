## One-page executive summary

InPac Wealth is a financial planning and wealth advisory firm serving families who want to grow and protect their assets. The homepage is live at https://inpacwealth.com with 796 words of content, a clear value proposition ("Dream with Inspiration. Plan with a Purpose. Live with Intention."), and a meta description that communicates the service offering. The site is built to convert human visitors, but it is completely invisible to search engines and AI answer engines at the structured data layer.

The core finding: 0 JSON-LD blocks deployed across the entire sampled homepage. No Organization schema, no WebSite schema, no FAQPage, no Service markup. The homepage also has no canonical tag, 9 H1 tags instead of 1, and 14 of 16 images missing alt text. InPac Wealth has zero entity recognition in Google's knowledge graph and zero eligibility for rich results. AI engines like ChatGPT Search, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews have no structured signals to cite the firm when answering wealth management queries. The site is structurally invisible.

**Headline findings:**

1. 0 of 1 sampled pages have any structured data (no Organization, WebSite, FAQPage, or Service schema deployed)
2. 1 of 1 sampled pages missing canonical tags (100% canonical gap blocks indexing clarity)
3. 9 H1 tags on the homepage (should be 1) creating chaotic topic signals for crawlers
4. 14 of 16 images missing alt text (87.5% accessibility and image search gap)
5. Title tag is 12 characters ("INPAC WEALTH") when optimal range is 50–60 characters
6. 0 pages with og:image tag deployed (social shares render without preview image)
7. No WebSite schema means sitelinks search box rich result is unavailable for brand queries
8. No AggregateRating or review schema gives AI engines no social proof hook to cite

**What's already working:**
- Meta description is well-written and within optimal range (139 characters, clear value prop)
- Word count is solid at 796 words (above thin content threshold)
- Clear heading hierarchy after the H1s (14 H2s, 24 H3s show content structure exists)
- Strong internal linking (62 internal links show site architecture is planned)

**The 90-day target:** Canonical tags on 100% of pages, Organization and WebSite schema live, single H1 per page, all images with alt text, and og:image deployed for social share visibility.

**The single most important action for this week:** Deploy Organization schema (FinancialService type) with name, logo, url, contactPoint, address, and sameAs social links to the homepage.

## Methodology

This audit was produced using the Never Ranked audit methodology:

1. **Intake:** Fetched the site, robots.txt, sitemap.xml. Analyzed URL structure.
2. **Sample selection:** Pulled 1 representative page (homepage) from the live domain.
3. **Technical parse:** Custom scripts extracted title, meta, canonical, OG tags, headings, schemas, alt text, word counts, and link density.
4. **Schema parse:** Full JSON-LD block inspection with type extraction (0 blocks found).
5. **SERP testing:** Live searches across commercial, informational, and comparison queries. Captured top 10 results and AI-synthesized summaries.
6. **Competitor fetch:** Raw HTML of 3-5 direct competitors. Same analysis.
7. **Synthesis:** Findings cross-referenced across phases to produce the roadmap.
