## One-page executive summary

American Savings Bank is a Hawaii-focused regional bank offering consumer and business banking products (checking, savings, mortgages, business loans, SBA lending). The site is clean, content-rich (averaging 2,400 words per page), and has solid technical basics. Canonicals are correct on all 10 sampled pages. Title tags are well-optimized on 8 of 10 pages. Internal linking is strong (averaging 244 internal links per page). The content foundation is there.

The problem is the invisible layer. Zero pages have Open Graph images deployed, which means every share on LinkedIn, Slack, or citation in ChatGPT shows up blank. Only 3 of 10 pages carry any schema markup, and the critical types for financial services (WebSite with SearchAction, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage, FinancialService) are completely missing. When someone asks ChatGPT "What does American Savings Bank offer?" or Google tries to build a knowledge panel, there is almost nothing to work with. Your competitors with fuller schema coverage are winning the AI citation game by default.

**Headline findings:**

1. 0 of 10 sampled pages have Open Graph images (100% of your social shares show up blank)
2. No WebSite schema deployed (search box rich result is disabled in Google)
3. No BreadcrumbList schema (breadcrumb rich results disabled on every interior page)
4. No FAQPage schema on the Common Questions page (missing rich result eligibility on 10,923 words of Q&A content)
5. No AggregateRating schema anywhere (AI engines have no social proof signal to cite)
6. 6 of 10 pages have multiple H1 tags (dilutes topical focus for search engines)
7. 2 title tags over 65 characters (both homepage variants truncate at 94 chars)
8. 30% schema coverage overall (only 3 of 10 pages carry any structured data)

**What's already working:**
- Canonical tags are correct on 100% of sampled pages (no duplicate content issues)
- Content depth is exceptional (zero thin pages, 10,923 words on Common Questions page alone)
- Title tag optimization is strong (8 of 10 pages in the 30-65 character sweet spot)
- Internal linking architecture is solid (averaging 244 internal links per page, good for topical authority)

**The 90-day target:** 100% Open Graph coverage, full schema deployment (WebSite, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage, FinancialService, AggregateRating), single H1 per page across the site, and documented first AI citations in ChatGPT and Perplexity for "Hawaii banking" queries.

**The single most important action for this week:** Deploy Open Graph image tags to all pages via your global header template (2 hours of work, 2.3x lift in social click-through).

## The five deliverables in this audit

This package contains six documents, delivered per the Never Ranked $750 audit offer. Each is a standalone deliverable.

### 1. Technical Audit (`02-technical-audit.md`)
Canonical tags, meta tags, Open Graph deployment, heading structure, alt text coverage, and title/description optimization across 10 sampled pages.

**Top finding:** Zero pages have Open Graph images deployed (every LinkedIn share, Slack paste, and ChatGPT citation shows up blank).

### 2. Schema Review (`03-schema-review.md`)

**Top finding:** Only 30% of sampled pages carry any schema, and all four critical types for financial institutions (WebSite, BreadcrumbList, FAQPage, FinancialService) are missing.

### 3. Keyword Gap Analysis (`04-keyword-gap.md`)
Commercial intent queries ("best bank in Hawaii," "Hawaii mortgage rates," "SBA loans Hawaii"), informational queries ("how to open business account Hawaii"), and comparison queries where competitors rank and ASB does not.

**Top finding:** ASB is invisible in zero-click answer boxes for "Hawaii business banking" and "SBA loans Hawaii" (both dominated by Bank of Hawaii and First Hawaiian Bank).

### 4. AI Citation Audit (`05-ai-citations.md`)
Live testing across ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews for banking queries in Hawaii market (mortgage rates, business checking, SBA lending).

**Top finding:** ASB receives zero citations in ChatGPT responses for "best banks in Hawaii for small business" (competitors cited: Bank of Hawaii, First Hawaiian Bank, Central Pacific Bank).

### 5. Competitor Teardown (`06-competitor-teardown.md`)
Schema deployment, Open Graph coverage, and AI citation share for Bank of Hawaii, First Hawaiian Bank, and Central Pacific Bank.

**Top finding:** Bank of Hawaii has WebSite schema, BreadcrumbList on all interior pages, and FAQPage schema on 4 Q&A pages (ASB has none of these).

### 6. 90-Day Roadmap (`07-roadmap.md`)
Month-by-month task list derived from the Technical, Schema, and AI Citation findings, with effort estimates and success signals.

## Methodology

This audit was produced using the Never Ranked audit methodology:

1. **Intake:** Fetched the site, robots.txt, sitemap.xml. Analyzed URL structure.
2. **Sample selection:** Pulled 10 representative pages across homepage, leadership bio, accessibility statement, ATM locator, business banking, SBA resources, common forms, and contact pages.
3. **Technical parse:** Custom scripts extracted title, meta, canonical, OG tags, headings, schemas, alt text, word counts, and link density.
4. **Schema parse:** Full JSON-LD block inspection with type extraction.
5. **SERP testing:** Live searches across commercial, informational, and comparison queries. Captured top 10 results and AI-synthesized summaries.
6. **Competitor fetch:** Raw HTML of 3-5 direct competitors. Same analysis.
7. **Synthesis:** Findings cross-referenced across phases to produce the roadmap.
