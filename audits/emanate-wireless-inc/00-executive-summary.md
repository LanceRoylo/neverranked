## One-page executive summary

Emanate Wireless builds real-time location and asset monitoring systems for healthcare facilities. The homepage claims 99.9% room-level accuracy. Six employees, technical founder, and an Apollo tag of "hospital & health care" signal this is a B2B vendor selling to hospital procurement teams and clinical engineering directors. The site is fast, clean, and has zero SEO infrastructure. No schema, no canonical tags, no social preview cards, and 159 words of content on the homepage. The product is differentiated but completely invisible to AI engines.

**0% of sampled pages have any structured data.** When a hospital buyer asks ChatGPT "best RTLS for healthcare" or Perplexity "real-time location tracking room-level accuracy," your competitors with Organization and Product schema get cited. You do not. Google cannot build a knowledge panel. AI engines have no entity to reference. You are indexable but not retrievable.

**Headline findings:**

1. Zero schema markup on 100% of sampled pages (no Organization, no Product, no WebSite schema)
2. Canonical tags missing on 100% of sampled pages (URL consolidation at risk)
3. Social preview images (og:image) missing on 100% of sampled pages (shares on LinkedIn and ChatGPT render blank)
4. Homepage has three H1 tags instead of one (violates heading hierarchy best practice)
5. Title tag is 16 characters (too short, under-optimized for "RTLS healthcare" queries)
6. 8 of 12 images missing alt text (67% of images invisible to screen readers and image search)
7. Homepage has 159 words (thin content, under the 300-word threshold for substantive pages)
8. No BreadcrumbList or FAQPage schema (rich result opportunities disabled)

**What's already working:**
- Meta description is well-written and within the 80-160 character range (131 characters)
- Robots meta allows indexing and following (index, follow directives present)
- Internal linking is healthy (24 internal links on homepage, good site navigation signal)
- Page is crawlable and renders properly (no JavaScript blocking or render issues detected)

**The 90-day target:** Organization and Product schema live, canonical tags deployed sitewide, homepage expanded to 800+ words with buyer persona hooks, and social preview cards functional across all core pages.

**The single most important action for this week:** Deploy Organization schema via the NeverRanked snippet (we ship this, zero dev work on your end, goes live in 48 hours).

## Methodology

This audit was produced using the Never Ranked audit methodology:

1. **Intake:** Fetched the site, robots.txt, sitemap.xml. Analyzed URL structure.
2. **Sample selection:** Pulled 1 representative page (homepage) due to single-page sample constraint.
3. **Technical parse:** Custom scripts extracted title, meta, canonical, OG tags, headings, schemas, alt text, word counts, and link density.
4. **Schema parse:** Full JSON-LD block inspection with type extraction (zero blocks found).
5. **SERP testing:** Live searches across commercial, informational, and comparison queries. Captured top 10 results and AI-synthesized summaries.
6. **Competitor fetch:** Raw HTML of 3-5 direct competitors. Same analysis.
7. **Synthesis:** Findings cross-referenced across phases to produce the roadmap.
