Methodology summary (both sides)

NeverRanked

7 AI surfaces measured daily for {{N_DAYS}} days: Perplexity, ChatGPT search, Gemini grounded, Microsoft Copilot via Bing, Google AI Overviews, plus Claude and Gemma for the model-knowledge layer.

18 hash-locked queries covering head intent ("best bank in Honolulu"), product-specific ("Hawaii bank for a first-time mortgage"), trust-signal ("most trusted bank in Hawaii"), value-conscious ("lowest checking account fees"), comparison ("ASB vs FHB vs BoH"), and long-tail ("startup", "real estate investor", "credit union").

Three repetitions per query per engine for noise control. Source-type classifier (9 buckets including review_directory, owned, competitor, independent_web). Within-citation depth (position-in-answer + sentiment context per recurring brand). Drift detection (day-over-day citation share movement, hash-locked).

Why this is anonymized

The subject brand is one of the seven Hawaii consumer banks named in the cohort. Naming the subject publicly creates trademark, trade-libel, and endorsement-implication risk that does not exist when the subject is unnamed. The methodology demonstration value is preserved by naming the cohort (these are publicly-competing brands, naming them collectively is honest), and the unredacted version is available 1:1 to qualified prospects. Same anonymization pattern is used in the Honolulu med-spa demo bundle for the same reasons.
