# Email templates — current product, honest voice

Pull from this file when a conversation needs a response. Every
template is locked to the post-retraction positioning and the
$4,500 + $1,500/mo pricing. Replace bracketed placeholders.
Voice rules apply (no em dashes, no semicolons in prose, no
AI tells, no formulaic openers).

If the situation does not match a template here, write fresh in
the same voice. Do not stretch a template that does not fit.

## What NOT to write in any reply

1. No "Hope you're well." No "Just following up." No formulaic
   openers. Specific first sentence or no opener.
2. No em dashes. None. Anywhere.
3. No semicolons in marketing prose.
4. No three-adjective lists ("fast, scalable, reliable").
5. No AI-tell phrases ("delve", "leverage", "robust",
   "comprehensive", "seamless", "elevate", "world-class",
   "in today's fast-paced").
6. No citation lift predictions. We measure. We do not predict.
7. No reference to retired products: Pulse, Signal, Amplify,
   Enterprise, $497, $2,000/mo, $750 audit, agency reseller
   program, the JavaScript snippet, "We DEPLOY THE FIX".
8. No "Hawaii Theatre score went from 45 to 95." That is the
   retracted causation claim. HTC is a capability reference
   only (Charity Navigator 2023, BBB 1999, missing Bing Profile,
   authority backlinks, meta description rewrites).
