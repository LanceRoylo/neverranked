# The canonical "what is NeverRanked" share email

When someone asks "wait, what does NeverRanked actually do these
days?" (a friend, a former contact, an investor, a peer founder,
a journalist, an agency who heard there was a pivot), paste this.

It links to every substantive public surface so the recipient can
follow whichever thread matters most to them. No replacement for
a real conversation, but it answers the question without you
having to type it again every time.

Replace `[Recipient first name]` with their name. Send.

---

> Subject: What NeverRanked is now
>
> [Recipient first name],
>
> Short answer: NeverRanked is a research engagement that
> measures what AI answer engines (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google
> AI Overviews, Microsoft Copilot, Gemini, Claude, Gemma) cite
> for a category. The deliverable is a forensic research memo
> plus a prepped punch list for the customer's team or agency
> to execute against. We do not do the execution work
> ourselves. $4,500 to kick off a category, $1,500 a month
> after for ongoing measurement, per category.
>
> Longer answer, if you have ten minutes:
>
> - **The story of why we rebuilt** (the kill-test we ran on our
>   own product, what we found, what we stopped selling):
>   https://neverranked.com/retraction/
>
> - **The methodology**, including a working example of what
>   we found in the Honolulu med-spa cohort:
>   https://neverranked.com/methodology/
>
> - **What a real $4,500 kickoff produces**, in full:
>   https://neverranked.com/example-engagement/
>
> - **For agencies**, if that's the angle that matters:
>   https://neverranked.com/for-agencies/
>
> - **For compliance/security teams**, the honest "we do not
>   touch your systems" page:
>   https://neverranked.com/security/

> - **What the first 30 days look like** for a new customer:
>   https://neverranked.com/first-30-days/
>
> - **The homepage** as the entry surface:
>   https://neverranked.com
>
> If any of this lands as relevant for someone you know, the
> warmest path is a five-query pilot on the category that
> matters to them. No cost, three to four days turnaround,
> they keep the output. Reply with the category and the
> competitors and I will run it.
>
> Lance

## Variants for specific situations

### When sending to someone who tracks the AEO category

Replace the first paragraph with:

> Short answer: we ran a pre-registered kill test on the
> snippet-based AEO thesis (zero of 409 citations on our own
> domain), published the retraction, and rebuilt around the
> measurement layer. The new product is cross-engine,
> cross-competitor, research-memo + punch-list. Most of the
> category still claims the snippet-to-citation causation we
> just disproved against ourselves. Worth showing you the data
> if you have not seen it.

### When sending to a journalist or analyst

Add at the end:

> If any of this is useful for something you are writing, the
> data and pre-registration file are public on GitHub and you
> are welcome to reproduce the kill test independently against
> any domain. Happy to walk through the methodology on a call.

### When sending to a founder peer (no commercial ask)

Replace the closing paragraph with:

> No ask here. Sharing because the rebuild was the most useful
> thing I have done in two years of running this and the
> public retraction post lays out the discipline change in case
> any of it transfers to what you are working on.

### When sending to an existing prospect who has gone quiet

Add a short opening line:

> Wanted to send a single update so you have the current state
> of the company in one place, not scattered across three
> previous conversations. Whatever decision you land on is
> good.

Then the standard body.

## When NOT to send this email

- To an active paying customer mid-engagement. They get the
  monthly delta memo, not a marketing share.
- To a journalist who has asked a specific question. Answer the
  specific question first.
- To someone who has explicitly asked us to stop messaging
  them. Respect the unsubscribe.
- To someone you have already sent this email to. Send a fresh
  reply against whatever they actually said.

## After sending

- Log who you sent it to (Apollo or whatever the current CRM
  surface is, per the outreach repo).
- If they reply, the email-template library at
  `meetings/kits/EMAIL-TEMPLATES.md` covers the next move for
  every reply shape.
- If they don't reply within two weeks, no follow-up. Sending
  this email IS the follow-up.
