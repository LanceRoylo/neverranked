# Retracted

This document was an explainer for NeverRanked's prior product:
a JavaScript snippet that was claimed to drive AI citations
(plus the agency reseller program built around it). We ran a
pre-registered kill test against our own domain. The result was
zero citations. The LLM crawlers do not execute JavaScript, so
the snippet was structurally invisible to them.

We stopped selling that product, retracted the public content
that promoted it, and rebuilt the company around the measurement
layer.

The current state of the company:

- **Product:** research engagement that measures what AI answer
  engines cite for a category.
- **Deliverable:** forensic memo plus a prepped punch list.
- **Boundary:** we do not execute. The customer's team or their
  agency ships the work.
- **Pricing:** $4,500 kickoff per category, $1,500/month per
  category ongoing. Per category, not per client.
- **Measurement stack:** seven AI surfaces daily. Five
  citation-grade engines that search the live web (Perplexity,
  ChatGPT search, Gemini grounded, Microsoft Copilot via Bing,
  Google AI Overviews) plus two model-knowledge engines (Claude,
  Gemma).

For the current honest pitch, see
[neverranked.com](https://neverranked.com).
